PromptHealth Ambassador Program

Open to the PromptHealth Community

Join our mission to help people connect with holistic wellness services and solutions based on individualized needs quickly and easily. Help spread our message of the importance of personalized wellness journey with your clients.

Eligibility Criteria

You must be signed up as a wellness provider/center OR company on our platform.

What you do

Stay engaged with the PromptHealth community:

  • Recommend and endorse those you like and trust in the PromptHealth community
  • Invite your colleagues to join the PromptHealth community
  • Create content on the PromptHealth community page, and share them to your other social media accounts using the share button.
  • If sharing on Instagram, use the hashtag #PromptHealthAmbassador with your post. Make sure to tag us @prompthealth!

What you get

We believe in the power of collaboration and want to help eachother out.

The more engaged you are, the more rewards you get. You can keep track of your credits in your dashboards. The credits can be used towards higher tier membership and/or social media services including being featured on our podcast, an interview via Instagram live, and a custom promotional video created by our social media team.

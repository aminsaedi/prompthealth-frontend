About Us

Prompt Health is a location-based application that connects people to Canadian accredited medical and holistic health services, based on individualized needs. Prompt Health is your online ‘personal assistant’ that takes the challenge out of accessing and navigating the care you need.

What makes us unique?

We do the screening or the “triage” for you using our algorithm to provide you with smart referrals, helping you to find your care with ease and confidence.

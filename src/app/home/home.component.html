About

Everybody has different health and wellness goals. You would typically ask around, lookup directories or Google search for different health professionals. These are called blind referrals as we are all different and one size does not fit all. With Prompt Health, people can now use one search engine to get smart suggestions that meet their health and wellness needs. They have the option to make it as personalized as possible based on their health risks, background and preferences. Using one easy to use platform and app, clients connect with practitioners, services and products at their convenience.

Our mission is to simplify access to care

Here at Prompt Health, we believe navigating care should be a lot better and easier in this digital era. The world is changing and so should the access to health and wellness solutions. At Prompt Health, we use technology to modernize and transform the client’s journey so they never have to guess again by connecting them to services based on their needs. We also provide technology for practitioners to elevate their client’s experience by facilitating continuous engagement.

What makes us unique?

Prompt Health connects clients with practitioners through intelligent questions that serve as a triage, which enables better and faster decision making for access to the most suitable solutions. We believe a need assessment can suggest better matches, which means better care. Prompt Heath also enriches provider-client interactions by creating rewards for engagement.

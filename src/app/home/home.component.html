About PromptHealth

Prompt Health is a location-based application that connects people to Canadian accredited medical and holistic health services, based on individualized needs. Prompt Health is almost like an online ‘personal assistant’ that takes the challenge out to access and navigate the care you need, easier and faster how and where you want it.

Our mission is to simplify access to care

At Prompt Health, we believe navigating care should be done with ease and with as much knowledge to empower stronger decisions for individual care. The world is constantly evolving, and so should the access to health and wellness solutions. Prompt Health uses technology to modernize and transform the client’s journey so they no longer have to blindly connect with services. This allows people to build health and wellness routines by connecting them to services based on their needs.

What makes us unique?

Every individual has different health and wellness goals; how we accomplish them is generally left up to each of us. Typically people ask around to friends and family, look up directories, or search on line for different health professionals. The results of those searches generally leave us with a list of unknown service providers based on someone else’s experiences and needs. We all have different needs and one size does not fit all. Prompt Health is here to change that experience to provide personalized options based on an individual’s specific health and wellness needs and goals. It allows people to compare and filter to narrow down searches to cater to their specific needs and how they want it. It is a service that is made and built for each individual.
Having worked as a clinician for over 15 years in one of the pioneer centers of Private Health, as well as providing business consultation to tech driven health related start-ups, I became acutely aware of the challenges of finding the right individual services for health and wellness. I believe we need to focus on strengthening knowledge to provide a system that helps both clients and small businesses in health and wellness connect to one another. That’s how Prompt Health came to life, a two sided online marketplace for individualized referral network in health and wellness”
